Darjeeling Community & Teaching Volunteers

Volunteer on childcare, community development & teaching projects in Gorubathan and Kalimpong in the Darjeeling region of West Bengal state, India. This volunteer project aims to serve poor, sick or otherwise underprivileged communities in the Darjeeling region by providing free education, childcare, medical assistance and other relief to those who are most in need, regardless of caste, creed, culture, race, gender, or religious background.
Teaching Projects: Volunteers teach basic English and other subjects in English, providing students with the chance to interact with a fluent speaker and improve their own English and also use your positive attitude to act as a motivator to encourage an love of learning within the students.
Childcare Projects: Volunteers will work at a Day Care Centre that aims to help poor communities in inaccessible or remote areas. The project provides free tutorial classes and other scholastic materials and services.
Community Outreach Projects: Volunteers will help with running awareness programmes in the local community on the issues surrounding HIV/AIDS, Healthcare, Human Trafficking, Child Labour and Drug Addiction in society.
